Meet **Morning Boost**—your quick, wholesome start to the day. This single-serve blend brings together hearty grains and seeds for steady energy, satisfying fiber, and essential nutrients.

**What’s inside**
- Amaranth — protein + minerals
- Barley — soluble fiber for fullness
- Chia - plant omega-3s + gel-forming fiber
- Flax seeds — soluble + insoluble fiber
- Hemp seeds — balanced omegas + protein
- Oats — heart-healthy fiber
- Quinoa — complete plant protein
- Sesame seeds — minerals like calcium
